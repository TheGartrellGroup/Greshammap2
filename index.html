Gresham Map2 - City of Gresham GIS Map Application

Welcome to the City of Gresham intranet GIS Web App! You can check parcel information, building permits, water lines, zonings and many other information through this application.

 Search

You can search a property by address, RNO (9-dijit property ID number), RNO6 (6-dijit property ID number), owner name, or State ID. Select a category from the search dropdown menu and type an input and press Enter. You can search by an address number and street name or just a street name.
You can also click any parcel on the map and property information will show up on the information window panel.

 Base Map

You can change the opacity of aerial image over the base street map.

 Information Panel

Click the information panel icon in the upper right-hand corner of the map to open the panel and turn on/off layers or see legends or search results.
    Layers
  • In the layers tab you can turn on or off layers you want to see on the map. You can also change the opacity of each layer group using the slider bar.
    Search Results
  • ‘TAXLOT Information’ shows basic parcel information from the City database.
  • ‘SunGard/HTE Information’ shows building permits and other information from the HTE databases.
  • ‘District Information from Metro' shows district information from Metro.
  • ‘More Information from County’ shows data directly from Multnomah County.
  • ‘Census Information’ shows data directly from Census Bureau Web services - American Community Servey (ACS) 2010-14 5-year Estimates.
Metro, Multnomah County, and Census data links are not affiliated with the City of Gresham and the City is not responsible for the content or privacy policies of this information.

    Legends
  • The legends tab shows the legends of layers that are turned on.

 Help

The help display explains how to use this map and contains other information, including disclaimer.

 Print

You can print the map with a selected paper size. When you click this button or choose one of paper layout, it will create a PDF file in a new winodw (or a tab) that you can print or save. Please make sure your browser allows pop-up window.

 Street View

The Google StreetView button changes your cursor, allowing you to click anywhere on the map to open the Google Street View window. If you click the Google StreetView button again, your cursor will return to its original shape. Please make sure your browser allows pop-up window.

 Identify

You can select any layer you want to idenity from the drop-down list and click 'Identify:none' button to start identifying the selected layer. When you are done, click the identify button again to change your cursor to its original shape.

 Measure

You can open the measurement window and choose three measutement options - area, distance and coordinates.

 Home

Home zooms out to the full extent.

 GeoLocation

GeoLocation allows you to share your location. The map will then zoom into your location.
